Discovery

The Discovery Phase in software development is a key initial step where an account executive assesses your project and its goals.. This includes evaluating technology feasibility, objectives, budget, and timeline. It starts with a call to understand your team and project needs. A summary of this call is reviewed by the product team to check technical and budget alignment. The outcome is a detailed proposal with features, budget, and a phased timeline. With this information, you can decide to proceed and place a refundable deposit to begin detailed scoping with a Project Manager.
